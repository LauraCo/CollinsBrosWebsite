Founded in 1968 by Mr. & Mrs. Clive Collins, Collins Brothers Pembs. Ltd. is still very much a family business, with Mr. Collins and two of his sons managing the ever-growing company.

We're based in the town of Pembroke and for the last near half a century we've continuously supplied other local companies, as thanks to our 'Customer Comes First' ethos they come back time and time again.

We currently stock over 400 pieces of machinery making us the largest single-depot Plant Hire business west of Llanelli, supplying small and medium-sized contractors as well as National Plant hire companies and County Councils west of Swansea and south of Aberystwyth.

Any queries, please do not hesitate to contact us.
